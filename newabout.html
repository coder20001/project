OUR JOURNEY

Our journey began with a simple goal: to create a seamless and efficient solution for residence maintenance management. From our humble beginnings, we've grown into a platform that empowers students, staff, and wardens to manage and track maintenance requests, assets, and work orders effortlessly. With a commitment to innovation and user satisfaction, we continue to evolve, ensuring that every residence can operate smoothly and efficiently, creating better living and working environments for everyone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Need something cleared up? Here are our most frequently asked questions

How do I submit a maintenance request?

You can submit a request by logging into your account and selecting the "Report Fault" option on the dashboard. Fill in the necessary details and submit the form.

What types of maintenance issues can I report?

You can report various types of issues including plumbing, electrical, infrastructure, and general repairs. Select the appropriate category when submitting your request.

How long does it take for a maintenance request to be resolved?

The resolution time varies depending on the nature of the issue. You can track the progress of your request under the "Work Order" section.

How do I know if my request has been approved?

Once your request is confirmed, you'll receive a notification via email and on your dashboard under the "Confirmed Requests" section.

What should I do if my maintenance request is urgent?

For urgent issues, please contact your residence warden directly for immediate assistance.
