Our Services

Reliable Services for All Your Residence Maintenance Needs

Maintenance Ticket Management

Allow students to submit maintenance tickets for issues they encounter in their residences. Enable users to track the status of their maintenance tickets.

Fault Category Management

Organize maintenance issues into specific categories (e.g., plumbing, electrical, structural) to help prioritize and assign tasks.

Feedback and Rating System

Allow students to provide feedback and rate the maintenance services. This helps in evaluating service quality and addressing areas for improvement.

Scheduling and Assignment

Schedule and assign maintenance tasks efficiently to ensure timely resolution of issues.

Role-Based Access Control

Implement role-based access control to ensure that users can only access and perform actions relevant to their role.

Reporting and Analytics

Generate reports on common issues, ticket resolution times, and maintenance trends to help improve service quality.
